Location

Central School Manor is located at 189 Elizabeth Street in Historic St Marys Ontario.

It is situated in a beautiful neighborhood with mature trees, parks, and stately Victorian homes.

The Manor is only a few short blocks away from the river walkway and historic downtown district, just a short stroll for all your amenities.
