The Building and Grounds

Central School Manor is truly a condominium like no other. Built in 1914 the building commands attention with its massive column and pediment entrances and its locally quarried limestone exterior. Built to last the test of time, this amazing structure has lent itself to be repurposed into the new contemporary needs of modern living. The historic charm of its architecture, setting and scale, provide an atmosphere of grandeur; a place you could proudly call home. Some of the newly added features to the general building and property include:

  • Indoor parking for 10 cars and outdoor parking for 17cars.
  • Professionally landscaped grounds with mature trees benches and cast iron gazebo.
  • A private courtyard garden exclusive to the residents of the Manor.
  • A common room featuring a general meeting room, kitchenette and a lounge with a fireplace.
  • Elevator for barrier free access to all 3 floors.
  • R20 Insulations to exterior walls.
  • Concrete floors for hydronic in floor heating and sound abatement.
  • Large storage lockers.
  • Intercom door security and control systems.
